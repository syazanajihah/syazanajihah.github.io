About Us

This company, K'late Pizza has been operated online since 2 years ago and now there are about 3 physical stores available in Malaysia, located in Kuala Lumpur, Selangor and Pulau Pinang. Hosted by 3 young women, Nur Zahirah bt Zamri, Nursyaza Najihah bt Zubir and Atiqah Hazirah bt Akmaluddin, this pizza company has achieved several awards in 2017, proving that K'late Pizza is one of the Malaysians' choice along with the other famous pizza company.

K'late Pizza offers 6 types of pizzas, beverages, combo set and also side orders.
