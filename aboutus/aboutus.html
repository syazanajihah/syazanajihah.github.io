ABOUT US

About Us

This company, K'late Pizza has been operated online since a year ago and now there is one physical store available in Malaysia, located in Gombak, Selangor. Hosted by 3 young women, Nur Zahirah bt Zamri, Nursyaza Najihah bt Zubir and Atiqah Hazirah bt Akmaluddin, this pizza company has achieved several awards in 2018, proving that K'late Pizza is one of the Malaysians' choice along with the other famous pizza company.

K'late Pizza provides variety of pizzas, beverages, side orders and combos for customers to choose from. There are 6 pizza flavours which are Pepperoni, Meat mania, Meatasaurus, Extravaganza, Vegie Galore and Baked Perfection.These pizzas came in 4 sizes, personal, small, medium and large. Customers can also choose their preferred pizza crust, which are newyork crust, flat bread, cheese and thin crust.

As for the beverages, K'Late Pizza offers 2 types of drinks which are bottle and can. There are mineral water, pepsi F&N ice cream soda and also F&N Orange.

As for the side orders, we provide Shawarma, Chicken roll, Macaroni Carbonara, Loaded Creampuff and Butter Cookie Cake. The recipes for all these pasta, pastry and deserts are our own recipes which we guaranteed the freshness taste of it.
